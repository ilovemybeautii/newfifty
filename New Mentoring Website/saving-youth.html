Kidz Korna's Vision

This New Mentoring Movement is under the umbrella of Kidz Korna, NFP. This initiative was created to focus on providing life-skills, wisdom, social and emotional support through mentoring to young individuals of challenging life styles. Kidz Korna was established in 1995 to create platforms for the safety and protection of young people with a priority in violence and abuse prevention through our Local Television Show, After-School Workshops, Community Events, Safe Haven Summer Projects and Educational Field-Trips.

Kidz Korna would like to support youth of all ages, in all stages with challenging lifestyles along with their parents, grand parents and guardians to offer knowledge, encouragement and friendship through THE NEW 50 CAMPAIGN. This year marks Kidz Korna's 20 year anniversary and to date our current project is to build a playground, outdoor family venue and community garden in Englewood, a blight neighborhood that's in despair.

If you would like to support Kidz Korna's latest project please donate online at: www.kidzkorna.com
Benefits are suggested from local fundraising, new mentoring community forums across the country.
